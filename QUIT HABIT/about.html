About the Project

Built to Help You
Break Free.

QuitHabit is a college software engineering project — a fully functional wellness platform to tackle three of the most common modern-day habit struggles.

🎯
Mission

Why We Built This

The average person spends over 6 hours a day on screens, consumes 20 teaspoons of added sugar, and struggles with habits they know they want to break but can't seem to.

QuitHabit was created to provide a simple, private, and honest tool for habit tracking — without subscriptions, ads, or social pressure. Just you and your data.

Built as a Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript project, everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

Why QuitHabit

What Makes It Different

🔒

100% Private by Design

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. No servers, no cloud accounts, no tracking cookies.

🚀

Zero Dependencies

Pure Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no libraries, no npm install required.

📊

Honest Analytics

See your streaks, relapses, and patterns without sugar-coating. Honest data drives real change.

🏅

Achievement System

Milestone badges from day 1 to month 1 — built on behavioural science to reinforce consistency.

📱

Fully Responsive

Works seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Open it in any browser, no install needed.

🆓

Permanently Free

No premium tier, no paywalls, no ads. QuitHabit is a passion project built for impact, not profit.
